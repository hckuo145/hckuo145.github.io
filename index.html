About Me

I am Heng-Cheng Kuo, currently a second-year Master's student in the Data Science Master Degree Program at National Taiwan University. I am working with Prof. Hung-yi Lee and Prof. Yu Tsao.

Prior to that, I received my B.S. degree in EE at National Taiwan University. I worked with Prof. Lin-shan Lee at the Speech Processing Lab.

My research interests include Anti-Spoofing, Speech Editing, and Self-Supervised Learning.
